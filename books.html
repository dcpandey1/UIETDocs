ABOUT THE BOOK

B.Sc. (Computer Science & Engineering/Information Technology) and M.Sc. (Computer Science/ Information Technology). This handbook is equally useful for the first year students of D.C.A., B.C.A. and M.C.A. The book has been written in very simple though organized language. The concept, underlying theory is explained thoroughly with proper diagrams and suitable examples. This book is surely going to be very useful for students and teachers as well.

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Dr. Ravindra Nath started his career as a research associate with IIM Lucknow. He also worked as a Programmer at LBS National Academy of Administration, Mussoorie. Later then he was lured into academic field. He has spent many happy years programming occasionally useful applications in a variety of languages as well as teaching engineers to do likewise. He has extensive experience of over 20 years in applying computers to problems in engineering design. He is the author of a number of research papers in peer journals and conferences on Computational Algorithms, Hidden Markov Model, Randomized algorithms and Optimization techniques. When not writing programming books or providing advice to others, he leads a life of leisure.Currently, he is the Director, Dean and Head Of Department (Computer Science And Engineering) of the University Institute of Engineering and Technology, CSJM University, Kanpur.
